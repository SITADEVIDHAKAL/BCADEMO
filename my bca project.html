About Our Department

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) department at University of Technology is committed to providing high-quality education in the field of computer applications. Our program is designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the rapidly evolving IT industry.


Established in 2005, our department has grown to become one of the most prestigious computer application departments in the region. We focus on a balanced approach that combines theoretical knowledge with practical application, ensuring our graduates are industry-ready.


Our state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and industry collaborations provide students with an enriching learning environment that fosters innovation and critical thinking.

Our Programs

BCA Regular

BCA Regular Program

A comprehensive three-year undergraduate program that covers fundamental and advanced concepts in computer applications, programming, database management, and software development.

BCA Honors

BCA Honors Program

An advanced program for high-achieving students that includes specialized courses, research projects, and industry internships to prepare for leadership roles in the IT sector.

MCA Integrated

Integrated MCA Program

A five-year integrated program that combines bachelor's and master's degrees in computer applications, providing in-depth knowledge and advanced specialization options.
